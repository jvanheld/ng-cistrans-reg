Samples

This section lists and describes each of the biological Samples under investgation, as well as any protocols that are specific to individual Samples.

Additional "processed data file" or "raw file" columns may be included.

Sample_nameAn arbitrary and unique identifier for each sample. This information will not appear in the final records and is only used as an internal reference. Each row represents a GEO Sample record. TitleUnique title that describes the Sample. Source_nameBriefly identify the biological material e.g., vastus lateralis muscle. OrganismeIdentify the organism(s) from which the sequences were derived. MoléculeType of molecule that was extracted from the biological material. Include one of the following: total RNA, polyA RNA, cytoplasmic RNA, nuclear RNA, genomic DNA, protein, or other. DescriptionType of molecule that was extracted from the biological material. Include one of the following: total RNA, polyA RNA, cytoplasmic RNA, nuclear RNA, genomic DNA, protein, or other. Processed_data_fileName of the file containing the processed data. Multiple 'processed data file' columns may be included when multiple processed data files exist for a Sample (as presented in EXAMPLE 1 worksheet).
